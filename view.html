Pasta Carbonara

Here's a step-by-step recipe for making classic pasta carbonara:
Ingredients:
Spaghetti: 400 grams (or another long pasta like linguine or bucatini)
Pancetta or guanciale: 150 grams, cut into small cubes
Eggs: 4 large (use 3 whole eggs and 1 yolk)
Parmesan cheese: 100 grams, grated (or Pecorino Romano for a more traditional touch)
Garlic: 1 clove, optional
Black pepper: Freshly ground
Salt: For the pasta water
Step-by-Step Instructions:
1. Boil the Pasta:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente according to the package instructions. Reserve about 1 cup of the pasta cooking water before draining.
2. Prepare the Sauce Mixture:
In a mixing bowl, combine the grated Parmesan cheese with the eggs. Beat until smooth and set aside.
3. Cook the Pancetta or Guanciale:
In a large skillet or frying pan, cook the pancetta or guanciale over medium heat. If desired, add a clove of crushed garlic for extra flavor and remove it once it becomes golden.
Cook until the meat is crispy and the fat has rendered out. Remove from heat to prevent it from burning.
4. Combine Pasta and Pancetta:
Add the drained spaghetti directly to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss until well-coated in the rendered fat.
5. Add the Egg Mixture:
Remove the skillet from the heat (to prevent scrambling the eggs) and pour in the egg and cheese mixture. Quickly toss the pasta, adding a bit of reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce. The heat from the pasta will cook the eggs gently.
6. Season and Serve:
Add plenty of freshly ground black pepper and toss again. Taste and adjust with more cheese or pepper if desired.
Serve immediately, garnished with additional grated Parmesan and black pepper.
Enjoy your delicious homemade pasta carbonara!

Chicken Stir-Fry

Quick Chicken Stir Fry Recipe:
1. Marinate Chicken (Optional): Mix sliced chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch; let sit for 15 mins.
2. Prep Veggies: Slice bell peppers, onion, carrot, and broccoli; mince garlic and ginger.
3. Cook Chicken: Stir-fry chicken in hot oil for 4-5 mins; set aside.
4. Stir-Fry Veggies: In the same pan, sauté garlic and ginger, then add veggies; cook 3-4 mins.
5. Combine & Sauce: Add chicken back, stir in stir-fry sauce and soy sauce; cook 2 mins.
6. Serve: Season if needed and serve over rice or noodles.
Enjoy!

Palak Paneer

Quick Palak Paneer Recipe: 1. Blanch Spinach: Boil 3 cups of spinach in hot water for 2 mins, then cool and blend into a puree. 2. Sauté Aromatics: In a pan, heat oil/butter, add 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 chopped onion, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste; sauté until golden. 3. Add Spices: Stir in 1 chopped tomato, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp cumin powder, and 1 tsp garam masala; cook until tomatoes soften. 4. Combine Spinach: Add spinach puree and simmer for 3-4 mins. Season with salt. 5. Add Paneer: Mix in 200g cubed paneer; simmer for 2 mins. 6. Finish: Stir in 2 tbsp cream (optional) and serve hot with naan or rice. Enjoy your homemade Palak Paneer!

Apple pomegranate oats

1. Prep: Peel and dice 1 apple; remove pomegranate seeds (about ½ cup). 2. Cook oats: In a pot, bring 1 cup of milk or water to a boil. Add ½ cup oats and reduce to a simmer for 5 minutes. 3. Add apples: Stir in diced apple and cook for 2-3 more minutes until softened. 4. Sweeten: Add 1 tbsp of honey or maple syrup and a pinch of cinnamon. 5. Top: Remove from heat and stir in pomegranate seeds. 6. Serve: Pour into a bowl, garnish with extra pomegranate seeds and a sprinkle of cinnamon if desired. Enjoy!

Lemon pepper salmon

Lemon Pepper Salmon Recipe 1. Preheat & Prepare: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with foil or parchment paper. 2. Season Salmon: Place salmon fillets skin-side down. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle lemon pepper seasoning, salt, and garlic powder evenly over the fillets. 3. Add Lemon Slices: Lay thin lemon slices on top of each fillet for extra flavor. 4. Bake: Place in the oven and bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork. 5. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve warm. Enjoy your delicious lemon pepper salmon!

Vermicelli noodles

Here's a simple step-by-step recipe for stir-fried vermicelli noodles: Ingredients: 200g vermicelli noodles 2 tbsp oil 2 garlic cloves, minced 1 small onion, sliced 1 carrot, julienned 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 100g cooked chicken or shrimp (optional) 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp oyster sauce Salt and pepper to taste Chopped green onions for garnish Instructions: 1. Soak noodles: Place vermicelli noodles in hot water for 3-5 minutes until softened. Drain and set aside. 2. Heat oil: In a pan or wok, heat the oil over medium-high heat. 3. Sauté aromatics: Add garlic and onions and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. 4. Add vegetables: Add carrots and bell pepper and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes. 5. Add protein: If using chicken or shrimp, add and stir for 2-3 minutes until heated through. 6. Add noodles: Toss in the drained vermicelli noodles. 7. Season: Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well to coat noodles evenly. 8. Garnish and serve: Sprinkle with chopped green onions and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade vermicelli noodles!

Poha

Here’s a simple step-by-step recipe for Poha (flattened rice): Ingredients: 1 cup poha (flattened rice) 1 tbsp oil 1/2 tsp mustard seeds 1/2 tsp cumin seeds 1 onion, chopped 1-2 green chilies, chopped 1/2 tsp turmeric powder 1/2 tsp sugar (optional) Salt to taste 1/4 cup peas (optional) Fresh coriander leaves, chopped Lemon juice (optional) Instructions: 1. Rinse poha: Wash the poha in a sieve under cold water for a few seconds. Let it drain. 2. Heat oil: In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. 3. Tempering: Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds, let them splutter. 4. Sauté onions and chilies: Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions soften. 5. Add spices: Stir in turmeric powder, sugar, and salt. 6. Add peas (optional): Add peas and cook for 2 minutes (if using). 7. Mix poha: Add the rinsed poha to the pan and mix gently. Cook for 3-4 minutes. 8. Garnish: Garnish with coriander leaves and lemon juice (optional). Serve hot and enjoy your Poha!

Rava Dosa

Here’s a simple step-by-step recipe for Rava Dosa: Ingredients: 1 cup rava (semolina) 1/4 cup rice flour 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (optional) 1/2 tsp cumin seeds 1/4 tsp black pepper 1-2 green chilies, chopped 1 tbsp grated ginger 1/4 cup finely chopped onions A pinch of asafoetida (hing) Salt to taste Water (for batter) Oil or ghee (for cooking) Instructions: 1. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, mix rava, rice flour, all-purpose flour (optional), cumin seeds, black pepper, chopped chilies, ginger, onions, and salt. Add enough water to make a thin batter (like buttermilk consistency). 2. Rest the batter: Let the batter rest for 15-20 minutes. 3. Heat the pan: Heat a non-stick or cast iron pan on medium-high and grease lightly with oil or ghee. 4. Make dosa: Pour a ladle of batter onto the hot pan. Spread it thin and unevenly in a circular motion (it should be lacy). 5. Cook: Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges. Cook until the edges turn crispy and the top looks set (2-3 minutes). 6. Flip and cook (optional): If desired, flip the dosa and cook for another 30 seconds.
Serve hot with chutney and sambar!
